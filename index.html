School Infrastructure Damage Assessment Survey

Department of Education and World Bank approach KLL

The Department of Education and The World Bank ask KLL to support an assessment of damage to schools because of KLL’s expertise in mobile data collection technology.

July 13, 2015

Pilot: Survey in Padmodaya School using KLL Collect SIDA app

Pilot survey conducted using KLL Collect SIDA App in Padmodaya School, Kathmandu.


July 21, 2015

Phase I: First training to Enumerators

KLL Collect SIDA App (version1) Training in Bhaktapur. The enumerators are trained on mobile device basics, and operating KLL Collect SIDA app.

September 23, 2015

Phase I: Data collection starts in 8 earthquake affected districts

SIDA Phase I Data Collection starts in 8 earthquake affected districts: Ramechhap, Kavrepalanchowk, Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur, Okhaldhunga, Makwanpur, Sindhuli).

September 30, 2015

Phase II: KLL Collect SIDA App ver 2 training at World Bank

KLL Collect SIDA App (version 2) Training at the World Bank. The enumerators are trained on added app features: quicker gps fix, text-photo split, survey statistics, photo compression).

November 16, 2015

Training to Department of Education officials

DoE (Department of Education) officials are trained on using KLL Collect SIDA app.

December 24, 2015

Phase II: SIDA orientation

The enumerators are introduced to the SIDA Phase II activities and timeline.

March 29, 2016

Phase II: Data collection started in remaining 6 districts

SIDA Phase II Data Collection starts in remaining 6 earthquake affected districts: Nuwakot, Rasuwa, Gorkha, Dhading, Sindhupalchok and Dolakha).

April 3, 2016

SIDA phase I results and findings dissemination

The data and findings of SIDA Phase I are handed over to the DoE (Department of Education) and UGC (University Grants Commission).

April 20, 2016
